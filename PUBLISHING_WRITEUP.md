# The Pharhad Road - Publishing And Marketing Writeup

Last reviewed: May 18, 2026
Studio: Teymurian Studios
Engine: Godot 4.6
Use: Steam page, itch.io page, website copy, trailers, screenshots, creator pitches, and playtest notes.

## Current Positioning

The Pharhad Road is a compact turn-based tactical RPG about surviving one connected fantasy battlefield. Enemies do not wait politely in isolated rooms. They wake when they see you, then the road starts turning into a fight faster than the party can comfortably handle.

Best store hook:

> Enemies wake when they see you. One careful step can control the encounter. One bad step can wake the road.

The build now has a stronger "tactical toy box" identity:

- Play Heroes: command Vanguard, Wayfinder, Ember Sage, and Becky across the road.
- Play Monsters: play the active monster side as the heroes advance. Flesh Beasts remain AI-driven pressure pieces.
- Play Pharhad: play with Pharhad's intervention powers and bend the battle from above.
- Send Cleric Z: start a solo Cleric Z run, then convert enemies with Love and Truth.
- Hero Chess: jump into a faster tactical board fight with blue player frames, red enemy frames, rivals, monsters, and rival salve vials.
- Optional 11s mode: player-controlled turns auto-end after 11 seconds, with audio countdown pressure. The button has no special color when off and turns red when on.
- Autosave restart: after Game Over, players can restart from the last saved run when one exists.
- Tutorial toggle, email feedback button, clearer turn queue highlights, detailed skill explanations, glowing ally markers, and richer in-game log hints.

Do not market the game as a D&D product, Forgotten Realms product, Phandalin adventure, official tabletop adaptation, or official SRD module. The safer pitch is original tactical fantasy with dice-driven combat, line-of-sight activation, and one connected road.

## Steam Store Copy

### Short Description

Lead four heroes across a connected tactical road where enemies wake through line of sight. Control the fight carefully, or switch into Play Monsters, Play Pharhad, Send Cleric Z, and Hero Chess for alternate tactical chaos.

### About This Game

The Pharhad Road is a compact turn-based tactical RPG about surviving one dangerous route instead of clearing disconnected arenas.

Enemies wake when they see you. A careful move can isolate one fight. A careless move can pull half the road into the same battle.

Command four distinct heroes:

- Vanguard: a durable front-line anchor with Cleaver Strike, Bolt Launcher, Grit Mend, and Tempo Break.
- Wayfinder: a scout and ranged controller with Quick Blade, Trail Bow, Quarry Brand, and Field Mend.
- Ember Sage: a fragile arcane striker with Spark Touch, Ember Shot, Inner Flare, and Homing Sparks.
- Becky: fast flying support with Two Kicks, Spear, Tempo Break, and Field Mend.

Every resource matters. Food refreshes key powers, Safe Camp restores the party, Pharhad Idol revives fallen heroes, Salve Vials save bad turns, and dropped weapons can change a run.

### More Ways To Play

- Play Heroes: lead the party across the road.
- Play Monsters: take over active monsters and turn the road against the heroes.
- Play Pharhad: step in as Pharhad when the battle needs a miracle.
- Send Cleric Z: enter alone with Love, Truth, and the chance to convert enemies into glowing green allies.
- Hero Chess: fight a quicker tactical showdown against rival heroes and monsters.
- 11s mode: add a real-time pressure clock to player turns.

### Feature Bullets

- Grid-based turn tactics with readable dice rolls.
- Line-of-sight enemy activation that rewards careful scouting.
- Four asymmetric heroes with melee, ranged, magic, healing, movement, and tempo tools.
- Monster roster with Thistlekin Scouts, Thistlekin Captains, Thistlekin Generals, Flesh Beasts, and Cave Brutes.
- Scarce recovery through Food, Salve Vials, Safe Camp, and Pharhad Idol.
- Downed-state fate checks and clutch stabilizing decisions.
- Detailed combat log explanations for selected skills.
- Autosaves with a Game Over option to restart from the last save.
- Alternate modes for playing heroes, active monsters, Pharhad, Cleric Z, or Hero Chess.

### Suggested Steam Tags

Turn-Based Tactics, Tactical RPG, Strategy RPG, Grid-Based Movement, Fantasy, Singleplayer, Turn-Based Combat, Party-Based RPG, Tactical, Strategy, RPG, Difficult, Dice.

## Trailer Direction

The first 10 seconds should show actual gameplay clearly. Steam viewers skim quickly, so open with a move that wakes enemies.

Recommended 45-60 second structure:

1. Show the road and a careful hero move.
2. Show enemies waking through line of sight.
3. Show Vanguard, Wayfinder, Ember Sage, and Becky acting in fast cuts.
4. Show a bad pull where too many enemies wake up.
5. Show items: Salve Vial, Spear, Food, Safe Camp, and Pharhad Idol.
6. Show Play Monsters with active monsters taking turns.
7. Show Play Pharhad intervention.
8. Show Send Cleric Z converting enemies with Love and Truth.
9. Show Hero Chess with blue player markers and red enemy markers.
10. End on the title, "Control the road, or let the road control the fight."

## Legal And Brand Safety

- Do not use D&D, Forgotten Realms, Phandalin, Lost Mine of Phandelver, Wizards logos, official art, official maps, or official trade dress in store materials.
- Do not imply affiliation with Wizards of the Coast or any official tabletop brand.
- Keep class, skill, enemy, and place names original.
- If any SRD text is intentionally used, include required attribution. The safer route for marketing copy is original plain-language descriptions.

## Steam Long Description

Survive The Pharhad Road.

The Pharhad Road is a compact turn-based tactical RPG about controlling one dangerous connected battlefield.

Enemies wake when they see you. A careful move can isolate one fight. A careless move can pull half the road into the same battle.

Command four heroes with distinct tactical jobs:

- Vanguard holds the front line with Cleaver Strike, Bolt Launcher, Grit Mend, and Tempo Break.
- Wayfinder scouts, marks targets, supports allies, and pressures enemies at range.
- Ember Sage is a fragile arcane damage dealer with Spark Touch, Ember Shot, Inner Flare, and Homing Sparks.
- Becky is fast support who can knock enemies down with Two Kicks, throw a Spear, boost tempo, and mend allies.

Control engagements:

- Move through a continuous route, not isolated rooms.
- Use line of sight to decide when enemy groups join the fight.
- Pull one group at a time or risk a fight that grows faster than you can stabilize.

Survive the run:

- Dice-driven attacks create critical hits, misses, clutch saves, and painful spirals.
- Food, Field Mend, Salve Vials, Safe Camp, Pharhad Idol, Help, and dropped weapons all matter.
- Downed heroes face fate checks, so every recovery decision has weight.
- Autosaves let you restart from the last saved run after Game Over when a save exists.

More ways to play:

- Play Heroes: lead the party across the road.
- Play Monsters: take the enemy side and turn the ambush around.
- Play Pharhad: intervene from above when the battle needs a miracle.
- Send Cleric Z: start alone, then convert enemies with Love and line-of-sight Truth.
- Hero Chess: jump into a faster tactical board showdown with heroes, rivals, and monsters.
- 11s mode: add a fast pressure timer to player-controlled turns.

Enemies:

- Thistlekin Scouts pressure from range.
- Thistlekin Captains hold the fight together.
- Thistlekin Generals make the road nastier.
- Flesh Beasts punish poor spacing and can knock heroes prone.
- The Cave Brute is the heavy wall at the end of a bad plan.

For players who like:

- Turn-based tactics with clear positional consequences.
- Small-party RPG combat.
- Resource pressure across a connected map.
- Runs where one risky step can change everything.

Session length:

- First run: 45-90 minutes.
- Replay runs: 25-45 minutes.
- Hero Chess: shorter tactical sessions.

Control the road, or let the road control the fight.
