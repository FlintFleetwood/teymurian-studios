Built for tactical choices

The Pharhad Road mixes readable dice with clean digital tactics: line-of-sight activation, movement ranges, action economy, ranged attacks, healing, enemy conversion, autosave restarts, and recovery decisions between fights.

Four hero roles

Command Vanguard, Wayfinder, Ember Sage, and Becky. Each brings distinct tools such as Grit Mend, Tempo Break, Quarry Brand, Field Mend, Inner Flare, and Homing Sparks.

Four-road campaign

Clear Road 1 through Road 4. Finish an encounter, recover, autosave, then continue. If Game Over appears and a save exists, restart from the last autosave.

Fog and line of sight

Enemies wake when the party reveals them by sight or nearby pressure. Scouting matters, and rushing forward can turn a small fight into a dangerous battle.

Multiple ways to play

Play Heroes, Play Monsters, Play Pharhad, Send Cleric Z, or enter Hero Chess: a faster tactical duel against rival heroes and monsters.

Readable dice combat

Attacks use readable dice notation, hit rolls, critical hits, healing rolls, saves, misses, advantage, special effects, and clear combat log feedback.

Resource survival

Food, Safe Camp, Salve Vials, Field Mend, Help, dropped weapons, and the Pharhad Idol create meaningful choices about when to recover and when to press forward.

Quick pitch

The Pharhad Road is a compact fantasy tactics game from Teymurian Studios. It is designed for players who enjoy grid movement, party roles, tactical positioning, and dice-based risk without needing a giant RPG campaign.

  • Turn-based grid combat
  • 32x32 tactical map movement
  • Fog of war and enemy activation
  • Playable Heroes, Monsters, Pharhad, Send Cleric Z, and Hero Chess
  • Optional 11s mode for fast pressure turns
  • Autosave restart after Game Over
  • Built in Godot

Player Manual

Learn the road before you step onto it. The official color manual explains the quick start loop, menu modes, turn queue, fog, 11s mode, hero party, enemies, items, skill encyclopedia, and practical road wisdom.

Quick Start

Pick a mode, choose a road, take turns, clear encounters, recover, autosave, and continue toward Road 4.

Hero Party

Review Vanguard, Wayfinder, Ember Sage, and Becky, including HP, movement, guard, initiative, and core skills.

Road Wisdom

Use bonus actions before attacks, stay close enough to heal, respect opportunity attacks, revive at Pharhad Idol first, then use Safe Camp.

Teymurian Studios

Teymurian Studios is an independent game studio focused on clear, character-driven tactics games. The studio’s goal is to make compact strategy experiences with personality, readable rules, and memorable fantasy encounters.

First release focus

Brand tone
